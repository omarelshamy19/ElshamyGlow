Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping at ElShamyGlow

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your shipping details and confirm your order. It's that easy!

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders across Egypt. Pay when you receive your products — no upfront payment needed.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days within Egypt, depending on your location. Cairo and major cities usually receive orders within 2–3 days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over 500 EGP. Orders below 500 EGP have a small delivery fee.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. We source directly from authorized distributors of L'Oréal, Maybelline, Nivea, and all other brands we carry. 100% authentic guaranteed.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Contact us to initiate a return.

Shipping Information

Fast and reliable delivery across all of Egypt

Delivery Time

Orders are processed within 24 hours. Delivery takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. Cairo and major cities: 2–3 days. Other regions: 3–5 days.

Shipping Costs

Free shipping on orders over 500 EGP. Orders under 500 EGP have a flat delivery fee of 30 EGP.

Coverage Area

We deliver to all governorates in Egypt: Cairo, Alexandria, Giza, Sharqia, Dakahlia, Gharbia, and all other regions.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation message with a tracking number. You can track your order on our Track Order page.

Returns Policy

Hassle-free returns within 7 days

Return Period

You may return any product within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.

How to Return

Contact us via our Contact page or WhatsApp. We'll guide you through the process and arrange for pickup if needed.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned product. The amount will be refunded to your original payment method.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately. We'll replace it at no extra cost.
